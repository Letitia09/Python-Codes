You are given a number N, your task is to check whether the number is GOOGLY or NOT GOOGLY .
A number is  GOOGLY if the um of digits of that number is prime.

Input1:
21

Output1:
GOOGLY

Explanation1:
the sum of the digits of 21 is 2+1=3 and 3 is a prime number.Hence, the output is GOOGLY


Input2:
12

Output2:
NOT GOOGLY

Explanation2:
the sum of the digits of 13 is 1+3=4 and 4 is not a prime number.Hence, the output is NOT GOOGLY

Input2:
212

Output:
GOOGLY

the sum of the digits of 212 is 2+1+2=5 and 5 is a prime number.Hence, the output is GOOGLY
